Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about roof replacements, inspections, bids, and how we work.

Should I get multiple bids before replacing my roof?

Yes. We actually encourage it. Get your other quotes first, then call us last. Homeowners who shop around usually notice the difference when they're talking directly to the owner instead of a commissioned sales rep.

Will I be dealing with a sales rep or the owner?

You'll be dealing directly with Craig. He takes the call, does the inspection, writes the bid, and stands behind the work. That's a big part of why our estimates stay straightforward and why homeowners feel comfortable calling us back.

Do you pull permits and register with cities when required?

Yes. Any city that requires registration or permits, we handle that. We do the work the right way, not the fastest way, because that's what protects you and your home long after the job is done.

Do you work with my insurance company?

Yes, when storm damage is legitimate and a claim makes sense. We work directly with your insurance adjuster to document damage and support the claims process, but we never pressure homeowners into filing claims they don't need.

What kind of shingles do you use on roof replacements?

We primarily install Owens Corning roofing systems, including impact-resistant shingles that are built for North Texas weather. We also upgrade the underlayment, ridge caps, and ventilation so the whole roof system performs the way it should.

What areas do you serve?

Coppell is home base, and a lot of our work comes from Coppell, Carrollton, Lewisville, Flower Mound, The Colony, and Plano. We also take projects across the wider DFW Metroplex, especially when it's a good fit for roof replacement, repair, or storm restoration.
