Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers from a contractor who's been in your neighborhood since 2010.

How do I know if my roof has storm damage?

Many homeowners can't tell from the ground — especially after hail events. Damage can include cracked or bruised shingles, missing granules, bent or damaged flashing, and dented gutters or vents. The safest approach is a professional inspection. We offer free inspections with no obligation, so there's no reason to guess.

Do you work with my insurance company?

Yes — when damage is present and a claim is appropriate, we work directly with your insurance adjuster to document damage and support the claims process. We never pressure homeowners into filing claims, and we never offer promises like a "free roof" — those are illegal and a major red flag. Our job is to be honest with you about what your roof actually needs.

What kind of shingles do you use?

We primarily install Owens Corning TruDefinition Duration Shingles, which are available in impact-resistant Class 3 and Class 4 ratings. Class 4 impact-resistant shingles can qualify homeowners for discounts on their homeowners insurance. We also upgrade the underlayment (ProArmor Synthetic) and ridge caps on every replacement — not just the shingles.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in a single day. Larger or more complex roofs may take two days. We stage materials in advance, work efficiently, and leave your property clean before we leave. We understand you have a life to get back to.

Why should I be careful about storm chasers?

After major storms, out-of-town contractors flood the area knocking on doors. Many use high-pressure tactics, ignore no-soliciting signs, and make illegal promises like "free roofs." They may disappear after collecting a down payment. We don't chase storms or knock on doors — the majority of our business comes from referrals. If a contractor showed up uninvited after a storm, be cautious.

Do you only do roofing, or can you help with other projects?

We're a full-service general contractor. In addition to roofing, we build and repair fences, install gutters and gutter guards, build custom patios and pergolas, and handle siding and general exterior work. If you have a project at your home, it's worth giving us a call.
